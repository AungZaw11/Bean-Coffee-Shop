Espresso Machine

The espresso machine is essential for brewing rich, concentrated coffee. It uses pressure to force hot water through finely-ground coffee beans.

Usage Tips:

  • Use fresh, high-quality coffee beans.
  • Ensure the water temperature is between 90-96°C.
  • Clean the machine regularly to maintain optimal performance.

French Press

The French press is a simple and effective way to brew coffee. It allows the coffee grounds to steep in hot water, resulting in a full-bodied flavor.

Usage Tips:

  • Use coarsely ground coffee for best results.
  • Let the coffee steep for 4 minutes before pressing.
  • Pour immediately after pressing to avoid bitterness.

Chemex Coffee Maker

The Chemex is an elegant pour-over coffee maker that brews clean, flavorful coffee. Its unique design also acts as a serving vessel.

Usage Tips:

  • Use Chemex filters for optimal filtration.
  • Pour water in a circular motion for even extraction.
  • Experiment with different grind sizes for varying flavors.

Coffee Maker Hand Tools

The portafilter holds coffee grounds, facilitates water flow, ensures even extraction, and produces rich espresso with balanced flavor.

Usage Tips:

  • Place a paper filter into the basket and fill the water reservoir with filtered water to the fill line.
  • Measure 14 g of coffee beans per serving.
  • Grind the coffee coarsely — it should have the same texture as sea salt.
  • Place the coffee into the filter, turn on your machine, and wait for the coffee to brew.

Coffee Brewer

A coffee brewer heats water, extracts coffee, filters grounds, keeps it warm, and offers programmable settings for convenience and consistency.

Usage Tips:

  • Pour water into your coffee maker's reservoir.
  • If using pre-ground coffee skip to Step 3.
  • Insert filter into the basket and pour your coffee into the filter.
  • Start the brew cycle.
  • Allow brew cycle to finish, then immediately discard the coffee grounds

Clever Dripper

The Clever Coffee Dripper features the form factor of a traditional pour-over setup and the function of an immersion brewer. Its built-in valve gives you full control over your coffee’s steeping time and intensity while the paper filtration ensures you get a clean, grit-free cup.

Usage Tips:

  • Weigh Your Beans. The ratio of coffee to water should be 1:16.
  • Grind Your Coffee. Just before brewing, grind your coffee beans.
  • Boil & Prep your Dripper.
  • Get your water up to temperature (195-205F) and place a filter into the Clever Dripper.
  • Pour Your Water and Stir The Crust.
  • Draw Down.
